### Character conflict
For PVP actions (including damaging actions, humiliating actions, stealing, taking items without other characters noticing), the enthusiastic consent of all players involved is needed.

Note: This does not cover being caught in area of effect spells (as long as it's a side effect, not the intention), accidental triggering of traps, etc.

### Potion drinking
Drinking a potion is a bonus action, but a full action can be taken for a healing potion to heal the full potential value, instead of rolling
